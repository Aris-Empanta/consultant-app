AVAILABLE APPOINTMENTS:
- They will be generated using the available hours intervals, the chosen appointment duration and the chosen break between appointments.
- The chosen appointment duration can receive values: 15, 30, 45, 60 
- The chosen break between appointments can receive values: 0, 15, 30, 45, 60

CANCELATIONS:
- The lawyer can modify/delete the available hours. 
- We will consider the relationship eith the appointments.
- If the appointment is less than 3 hours from now, he cannot cancel it. 
- He can cancel appointments. Once he does, the available appointments will be updated.
- The appointments can be cancelled by the lawyer 3 hours before at least. So do the available hours .
